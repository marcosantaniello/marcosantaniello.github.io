I am an economist with a passion for uncovering the stories hidden within the numbers. I studied political economy at King's College London and Hong Kong University and hold an MPhil in economics from the University of Oxford. In my master's thesis, I investigated whether investments in large-scale transport infrastructure foster the development of connected areas using a network of least-cost paths. Previously, I made use of syntetic controls to study the impact of collective wage bargaining on countries' macroeconomic performance in my bachelor's dissertation.
