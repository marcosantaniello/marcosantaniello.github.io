Marco Santaniello

E-mail CV LinkedIn GitHub

I am a Ratings Associate in Moody's Sovereign team, interested in international economics and political economy. I studied political economy at King's College London and Hong Kong University and hold an MPhil in economics from the University of Oxford. In my master's thesis, I investigated whether investments in large-scale transport infrastructure foster the development of connected areas using a network of least-cost paths. Previously, I made use of synthetic controls to study the impact of collective wage bargaining on countries' macroeconomic performance in my bachelor's dissertation.
